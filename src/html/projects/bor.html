BOR arduino interaface board

During my freshman year, I was part of WPI's Battle of the Rockets team. Part of BOR involves creating a rover that will be launched in the rocket. I was tasked with creating a shield for the Arduino mega that would be controlling the rover. This included creating ports for all of the sensors and actuators that we were using on the rover. Unfortunately, the rover never flew as the team dissolved shortly before the competition.
