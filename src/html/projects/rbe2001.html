RBE 2001 Final Project

In this project, we were tasked with creating a robot, that utilized a 4-bar to lift plastic and aluminum plates to three different positions. Additanly, it had to maneuver around the field in order to accomplish these tasks. We spent the majority of our time working on the design of our 4-bar. Unfortunately, when manufacturing our 4-bar, we did not account for the repeated stress that was put on the mechanism. Because of this, our gear train became dislodged, and we where not able to perform a full demo. Upon completion of the project, we wrote a full technical report detailing our choices and the mistakes we made.
